Warm, optimistic retro-sci-fi adventure — a universe that feels handbuilt, well-loved, and slightly improvised. Surfaces are weathered sheet metal and molded panels with visible rivets, welded seams, chipped off-white and pale green paint over raw metal, rust streaks, and hand-stenciled markings. Nothing looks military, sleek, or threatening — every object could be a practical prop built by a creative set designer. Space itself feels inviting: deep indigo-blue with warm star clusters, not cold black void. Warm amber key light from the right, cool blue fill from the left, rich saturated colors especially teals and warm greys, gentle lens flares on light sources, subtle analog film grain with soft halation around highlights. Camera moves are smooth and cinematic, never shaky or aggressive.
